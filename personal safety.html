PERSONAL SAFETY

If you lose someone these are the things you can do:

- Contact a member of staff nearby
- Visit the tent in the middle of the festival which is there if you lose your friends or family
- Lost children can be cared for and looked after in the tent

In case of an emergency:
In the event of an emergency there are over 500 staff or stewards on duty whilst the festival is occurring, you will know who they are as they wear uniform. They should be able to help you or give you the response that is required.

Drugs Policy:
Battle of the bands does not condone the use of drugs. It is illegal to buy, sell or take drugs. Drugs are not allowed to be taken usually, so this applies to the festival aswell.
