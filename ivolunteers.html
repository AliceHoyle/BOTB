VOLUNTEERS

Volunteering is a great way to see a whole new side to the festival, you can see behind the scenes and all the work and effect that goes into the festival. You'll meet new people and learn how to work as a team. These are the things you can when you volunteer:

- A free ticket

- Time to see some amazing acts

- Lots of memories

For all volunteering, you'll need to be 18 or over, have proof to volunteer in the UK (contact the respective volunteer organisation or visit their website for more information) and be available to work the shifts allocated to you throughout the weekend.
